What is Uplabdh Nidhi Limited?

Empowering Financial Freedom for Our Members

Definition

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited is a member-driven non-banking financial company, recognized under Section 406 of the Companies Act, 2013. It facilitates borrowing and lending exclusively among its members, fostering a culture of thrift and mutual benefit.

Also known as Permanent Fund, Benefit Fund, or Mutual Benefit Company, it operates on the “Principle of Mutuality” and is regulated by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

Members Only Company

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited is established by the members and for the members. Only registered members can avail financial services. No external party can deposit, borrow, or influence its operations.

Loans We Offer

  • Secured Gold Loans
  • Property Loans
  • Loans against LIC / FD

All loans are offered at competitive rates, up to 20% interest. Legal recovery can be initiated for defaulted repayments.

Deposits We Accept

  • Fixed Deposits (FD)
  • Recurring Deposits (RD)
  • Savings Accounts

Attractive interest rates of up to 12.5% on FD & RD. Savings accounts offer up to 2% more than nationalized banks.

We accept deposits up to 20x of the invested capital.

What types of loans does Uplabdh Nidhi Limited offer?

We provide a variety of loan products, including Gold Loans, Personal Loans, Business Loans, and Emergency Loans tailored to meet our members' financial needs.

How do I become a member of Uplabdh Nidhi Limited?

You can become a member by completing our registration form, providing KYC documents (ID, address proof, photo, signature), and paying the nominal membership fee. Once approved, you'll receive your unique Member ID.

What is the process to apply for a gold loan?

Visit our branch or apply online, submit your gold for valuation, and provide your ID and address proof. Once verified, the loan is disbursed immediately based on the gold's value.

How secure is my personal and financial information?

We follow industry-standard data protection practices. All your personal data is encrypted and securely stored. We strictly follow RBI guidelines and Nidhi company regulations to ensure compliance and transparency.

What deposit schemes are available for members?

We offer various deposit plans including Fixed Deposits (FD), Recurring Deposits (RD), and Daily Deposit Schemes, each with competitive interest rates and flexible tenures.
