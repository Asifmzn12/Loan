UPLABDH Nidhi Limited Property Loan

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited offers Property Loans exclusively to its registered members, in accordance with the Nidhi rules. To avail of a loan against property, membership with Uplabdh Nidhi is mandatory.

Members can avail loans up to ₹2.0 lakh or 50% of the property value, whichever is lower. Our property loans are structured with attractive interest rates, easy processing, and minimal documentation, ensuring a hassle-free experience for our members.

✔ Salary Slip/Bank Passbook for the past 2 years

How to Apply

  1. Become a member of Uplabdh Nidhi Limited if you are not already.
  2. Visit your nearest Uplabdh Nidhi branch with your property documents and identification.
  3. Complete the application form with required documents for verification and approval.

Why Choose Uplabdh Nidhi Limited?

We offer member-focused financial solutions with complete transparency. Our Property Loan products come with competitive interest rates, flexible repayment options, and secure handling of your property documents. Our dedicated team ensures quick processing and personalized support at every step.
