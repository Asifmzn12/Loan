Fixed Deposit

Secure your savings with high-interest Fixed Deposits at Uplabdh Nidhi Limited.

One Time Investment for a Secure Future

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a unique term deposit designed for individuals seeking stable and secure returns. At Uplabdh Nidhi Limited, registered members can start a Fixed Deposit with just ₹1,000. The amount is deposited once for the selected tenure, and members earn quarterly compounded interest.

Our Fixed Deposit tenures range from a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 5 years, providing flexible options for all saving goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Fixed Deposits can be booked for a minimum of 6 months to a maximum of 5 years (60 months). Available options: 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 years.

₹1,000 is the minimum amount to start an FD, then in multiples of ₹1,000.

Interest rates range from 8% to 10.5% per annum, compounded quarterly.

  • No premature withdrawal for FDs ≤ 24 months.
  • For FDs > 24 months, 2% less than maturity interest + ₹200 processing charge.

  • After 24 months: Loan up to 50% of the deposit amount.
  • After 36 months: Loan up to 75% of the deposit amount.
