Privacy Policy of Uplabdh Nidhi Limited

Welcome to Uplabdh Nidhi Limited! Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data responsibly.

1. Introduction

This website www.uplabdh.in and the associated mobile app are operated by Uplabdh Nidhi Limited ("Company", "we", "our").

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with transparency and care.

2. Information Covered

This policy covers all personal data collected and processed by us to provide services. We only collect what is necessary.

3. Types of Personal Data We Process

Personal Information:
  • Name, email address, phone number, DOB, gender, etc.
  • Official documents such as PAN for KYC.
  • Financial info like account, mutual funds, loan details.
Usage & Location Data:
  • App usage, IP address, device data, location (if permission granted).

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to contact you, manage communication, improve services, and offer personalized features.

5. Data Sharing & Amendments

We may share your data (excluding passwords, medical, or biometric info) with authorized third parties. Our policy may update from time to time at uplabdh.in.

6. Your Rights & Consent Withdrawal

If you wish to withdraw consent, email us from your registered email at info@uplabdh.in.

7. Storage, Disclosure & Cookies

We retain data only as long as necessary. We do not sell your data. We use third-party cookies for better experience, which you can disable via browser settings.

8. Grievance Redressal

For any concerns, please contact our Customer Care team. Visit uplabdh.in for support details.

Still Have Questions?

Reach out to us by email and we'll respond as soon as possible.
