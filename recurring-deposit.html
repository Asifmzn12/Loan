Recurring Deposit

Grow your savings systematically with high-interest Recurring Deposits at Uplabdh Nidhi Limited.

Systematic Savings for a Better Future

A Recurring Deposit (RD) is a unique term deposit ideal for disciplined savings. At Uplabdh Nidhi Limited, registered members can open an RD starting with as low as ₹100. Members deposit a fixed amount monthly for a chosen tenure, earning cumulative returns with quarterly compounding.

Our Recurring Deposit tenures range from a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 5 years, ensuring flexibility and growth for every member's savings goals.
