Corporate Social Responsibility Of Uplabdh Nidhi Limited

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited has consistently embraced its social responsibility, aiming to uplift communities and empower individuals through various impactful initiatives. We are committed to creating sustainable development and improving the quality of life in the regions we serve.

We have implemented multiple programs focused on financial literacy, digital inclusion, healthcare, education, village development, and environmental conservation, designed to strengthen underprivileged segments of society, especially in rural India.

Financial Education

Our financial literacy initiatives aim to enhance decision-making capabilities among underserved and resource-poor communities. Through structured programs, Uplabdh Nidhi Limited has educated over 223,000 beneficiaries as of February 2021.

Delivery Models

We use a combination of classroom training, mass awareness campaigns, and digital tools. With support from Uplabdh Nidhi Limited, initiatives are operational in Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, and Uttar Pradesh.

Classroom Training

Training modules cover budgeting, saving, investment, banking, insurance, and more over 3 days, in small, interactive batches. In FY 2020-21, 30,134 people were trained.

Community-Based Approach

Mass awareness drives involve 50-200 villagers per session, using audiovisual aids to deliver knowledge on financial literacy and government schemes.

Special Initiatives

  • Financial Literacy Week: Celebrated with the theme "Consumer Protection" from Feb 10–15, 2020.
  • Dialogue on Business (DOB): Business skill training using games and real-life scenarios. Over 2,800 entrepreneurs benefited.
  • Digital Financial Literacy: Reached 62,474 individuals across Bihar and UP using custom tools like flashcards and games.

Healthcare Program

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited provides free primary healthcare through mobile van polyclinics and clinics in collaboration with hospitals. Over 43,800 patients have been treated across 15 locations in Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, and Uttar Pradesh.

Village Development Programme

We adopt a holistic approach to rural upliftment—assessing needs, implementing interventions, and engaging communities. Over 8,000 beneficiaries reached so far.

Plantation Drives: On June 5, 2020, 30 fruit-bearing saplings were planted in two villages to promote sustainability.

Learning Enhancement Programme

We support rural children (ages 5-12) with reading, numeracy, and creativity through structured programs. Active in 4 villages near Varanasi, currently educating 130 children.

Support for Special Groups

Regular engagement with orphanages and old-age homes reinforces our commitment to inclusive growth. Monthly visits and contributions foster dignity and care among residents.

Orphanages:

We support homes for abandoned and differently-abled children up to the age of 22, offering safe spaces and holistic care.

Old Age Homes:

We partner with shelters for the elderly, ensuring they live in dignity and comfort. Engagements include donations and wellness activities.
