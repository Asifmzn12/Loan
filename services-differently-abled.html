Services For Differently Abled

Services for Differently Abled Customers

Committed to providing inclusive and accessible banking experiences for all.

As per RBI circulars:

  • DBOD.No.Leg.BC.91 /09.07.005/2007-08 dated June 4, 2008
  • DBOD.No.Leg.BC.123/09.07.005/2008-09 dated April 13, 2009
  • DBOD.No.Leg.BC.38 /09.07.005/2012-13 dated September 5, 2012
  • DBOD.No.Leg.BC.113/09.07.005/2013-14 dated May 21, 2014
  • DBR.No.Leg.BC.96/09.07.005/2017-18 dated November 9, 2017

All banking services are mandated to be accessible to persons with disabilities.

Uplabdh Nidhi Limited is fully committed to ensuring that all its services are offered to differently abled customers without discrimination. We follow RBI guidelines, as reinforced by our internal circular RB/PSNs/CIR/4153, to provide fair access to all banking products and services.

Our websites, internet banking, and mobile applications are being aligned with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) for better usability.

Accessible ATMs have been set up following IBA Standards on Accessible ATMs, including tactile keypads, braille instructions, and audio navigation for visually impaired users.

If any differently abled customer experiences difficulty in accessing our services, they are encouraged to write to us at info@uplabdh.in or call our helpline at 1800-123-5871 for prompt redressal.
