Daily Deposit (DD)

Empowering members to save daily and grow wealth steadily with Uplabdh Nidhi Limited.

Small Daily Savings, Big Future Returns

A Recurring Deposit, commonly known as Daily Deposit (DD), is a unique term-deposit provided by Nidhi companies. At Uplabdh Nidhi Limited, this savings option is exclusively available to registered members. Members can start daily deposits with as little as ₹50, encouraging disciplined savings habits with attractive returns.

Members deposit a fixed amount daily for a chosen tenure, and the returns are compounded quarterly. The available tenures are 1 year and 2 years, ensuring flexibility and steady growth.

Daily Deposit FAQs

Daily deposits are accepted for a minimum of 1 year (12 months) and a maximum of 2 years (24 months).

The minimum investment starts at ₹50 per day, increasing thereafter in multiples of ₹10.

  • 1 Year – 8.00% per annum
  • 2 Years – 9.00% per annum
Interest is compounded quarterly.

  • No premature closure allowed within the first 6 months.
  • If closed between 3 to 6 months, no interest is paid.
  • Post 6 months, premature withdrawals earn 2% less than the maturity interest rate.

For 24-month schemes, a loan facility is available up to 75% of the deposit amount after completion of 12 months.

No, the interest rate is fixed and standard for all; no additional benefits for senior citizens or women.

A penalty of ₹2 per ₹100 per month is charged if installments are not deposited regularly.
